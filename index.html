About Project 2028

A progressive alternative to regressive policy platforms

Project 2028 is a forward-thinking initiative designed to counter regressive policy platforms with a positive, progressive vision for America's future. We believe in building a more equitable, sustainable, and just society that works for everyone, not just the privileged few.

Our comprehensive policy platform addresses the most pressing challenges facing our nation, from climate change and healthcare access to economic inequality and voting rights. Through research, advocacy, and grassroots organizing, we're working to create a better future for all Americans.
