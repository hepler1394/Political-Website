How You Can Help

There are many ways to contribute to Project 2028's vision

Volunteer

Volunteers are the driving force behind the Democratic Party and Project 2028. By dedicating your time and skills, you can help elect Democrats, advocate for progressive policies, and build a better future for all Americans. Join our grassroots movement today!

  • Organize Local Events: Bring your community together for change.
  • Make Calls & Send Texts: Reach out to voters in key elections.
  • Knock on Doors: Spread the word directly in neighborhoods.
  • Share & Create Digital Content: Amplify Democratic values online.
  • Support Campaign Operations: Help with crucial behind-the-scenes tasks.

Support

Financial contributions of any size help fund our research, advocacy, and organizing efforts. Your support enables us to develop comprehensive policy proposals, conduct outreach, and build the movement for progressive change.
