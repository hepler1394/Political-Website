Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities.

Last Updated: October 27, 2023

Project 2028 is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Measures to Support Accessibility

Project 2028 takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of our website (project2028.org):

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement where applicable.
  • Integrate accessibility into our content publishing practices.
  • Provide continual accessibility awareness for our staff where possible.
  • Assign clear accessibility targets and responsibilities where applicable.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA.

Project 2028 is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard yet. We are working to achieve greater conformance.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Project 2028's website. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers:

  • Contact: Via the methods listed on our Contact Us page.
  • We try to respond to feedback within a reasonable timeframe.

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of Project 2028's website relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.

Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility, there may be some limitations. We are working to address these. Please contact us if you observe an issue.

Assessment Approach

Project 2028 assesses the accessibility of its website through self-evaluation and aims to incorporate user feedback.

This statement was last updated on October 27, 2023.
