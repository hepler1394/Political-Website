Economic Justice

Building an economy that works for everyone

Project 2028 envisions an economy that generates prosperity for all Americans, not just those at the top. Our economic policies are designed to create good-paying jobs, reduce inequality, and ensure that everyone shares in America's growth.

Living Wages

Establish a $25 national minimum wage indexed to inflation and productivity growth, ensuring that all workers can afford basic necessities and participate in the economy.

Universal Basic Income

Implement a monthly UBI of $1,000 for all adult citizens, providing economic security and recognizing the value of unpaid work while stimulating local economies.

Worker Empowerment

Strengthen labor protections, guarantee paid family and medical leave, and ensure that workers have a voice in corporate decision-making through board representation.

Progressive Taxation

Reform the tax code to ensure that corporations and the wealthy pay their fair share, including a wealth tax on assets over $50 million and closing tax loopholes.

Climate Action

Securing a sustainable future for all

Project 2028 recognizes climate change as the existential challenge of our time. Our comprehensive climate policies will rapidly transition America to clean energy, create millions of green jobs, and ensure environmental justice for all communities.

100% Clean Energy

Transition to 100% clean electricity by 2035 and net-zero emissions economy-wide by 2045 through massive investments in renewable energy, storage, and grid modernization.

Green New Deal

Implement a comprehensive Green New Deal that creates millions of high-paying jobs while rebuilding America's infrastructure to be sustainable and resilient.

Environmental Justice

Ensure that frontline and vulnerable communities benefit first from the clean energy transition and receive resources to adapt to climate impacts.

Carbon Pricing

Establish a carbon fee and dividend system that puts a price on pollution while returning revenue directly to American households.

Education Reform

Investing in America's future

Project 2028 believes that quality education is a right for all Americans, not a privilege for the few. Our education policies will transform America's education system from pre-K through college, ensuring that every student has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Universal Pre-K

Guarantee high-quality, free pre-kindergarten for all 3 and 4-year-olds, setting every child up for success from the start.

K-12 Excellence

Triple Title I funding for schools in low-income areas, raise teacher pay by 50%, and modernize school infrastructure nationwide.

Free Public College

Make all public colleges, universities, and trade schools tuition-free, and cancel existing student debt for all Americans.

Lifelong Learning

Create a national system of free continuing education and skills training for all adults throughout their careers.

Technology & Innovation

Harnessing technology for the common good

Project 2028 envisions a future where technology serves humanity, expands opportunity, and solves our greatest challenges. Our technology policies will ensure that America leads in innovation while guaranteeing that the benefits are widely shared and potential harms are prevented.

Universal Broadband

Guarantee high-speed internet access as a public utility for every American household, business, and school, with particular focus on rural and underserved areas.

AI Governance

Establish a comprehensive framework for ethical AI development and deployment that protects privacy, prevents discrimination, and ensures human oversight.

Digital Rights

Create a Digital Bill of Rights that guarantees privacy, data ownership, freedom from surveillance, and protection from algorithmic discrimination.

Innovation Investment

Invest $2 trillion in R&D for breakthrough technologies in clean energy, medicine, computing, and space exploration through public research institutions.
