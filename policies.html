Economic Justice

Building an economy that works for everyone

Democrats are focused on building an economy from the bottom up and middle out, creating millions of jobs, including in manufacturing, and achieving record-low unemployment. The party is committed to tackling inflation by lowering living costs, making essentials like prescription drugs more affordable, and investing in American manufacturing and infrastructure to ease supply chain pressures.

Creating Good Jobs & Expanding Labor Rights

President Biden and Democrats are committed to creating good-paying jobs, expanding labor rights, and ensuring everyone has a chance to succeed.

Fair Tax System

Democrats believe in a fair tax system, where the wealthiest individuals and largest corporations pay their fair share. They are committed to reducing taxes for the middle class while ensuring that the ultra-wealthy and large corporations contribute to the nation’s economic growth.

Protecting Essential Programs

Democrats are committed to protecting Social Security, Medicare, and Medicaid, ensuring that millions of Americans who rely on these programs for their retirement, health care, and daily needs are not left behind. They are fighting to preserve these essential programs for future generations.

Lowering Costs for Families

Democrats are committed to tackling inflation by lowering the cost of living for American families. They have passed legislation to make prescription drugs more affordable and are working to reduce supply chain bottlenecks and ease inflationary pressures by investing in American manufacturing and infrastructure.

Climate Action

Securing a sustainable future for all

Democrats are taking aggressive action to combat the climate crisis, notably through the Inflation Reduction Act. These historic investments aim to lower energy bills for American families, strengthen energy security, create jobs, and advance climate goals. While building a clean energy future, the party is also focused on ensuring access to affordable energy, including working to reduce gas prices and expand domestic energy production.

Aggressive Climate Action

President Biden and Democrats passed the Inflation Reduction Act, which takes aggressive action to combat the existential crisis of climate change.

Benefits for Families & Job Creation

These historic investments will help American families save every year on their energy bills while strengthening our energy security, creating jobs, and getting us closer to meeting our climate goals.

Affordable Gas and Energy

As we build a clean energy future, we’re also ensuring families have access to affordable gas and energy.

Reducing Gas Prices & Domestic Production

The Biden administration worked with oil producers to help reduce gas prices and is focused on expanding domestic energy production.

Education Reform

Investing in America's future

Democrats are committed to ensuring every American has access to quality education and the opportunity to succeed. This includes efforts to make education more affordable and address the challenges of student loan debt, ensuring students are not overwhelmed by debt upon graduation.

Easing Student Loan Burden

Democrats are working to ease the burden of student loan debt, with measures to forgive loans for eligible borrowers.

Expanding Access to Affordable Education

Democrats are working to expand access to affordable education for all Americans.

Preventing Debt Overwhelm

Democrats are working to ensure that students aren’t overwhelmed with debt upon graduation.

Investing in Future Opportunities

Investing in education at all levels to provide pathways to future success for all Americans.

Technology & Innovation

Harnessing technology for the common good

Artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming our world, from the economy to social interactions. This progress brings concerns about data privacy, algorithmic bias, workforce impacts, and democratic integrity. A strong consensus calls for robust regulations and ethical guidelines. The Center for American Progress advocates for a comprehensive AI strategy to harness AI's opportunities safely while mitigating risks. This requires collaboration between government, the private sector, and civil society to ensure technology advances equitably and safely for all Americans.

Addressing AI's Impact

Addressing concerns of data privacy, algorithmic bias, workforce disruptions, and AI's impact on democracy.

Robust Regulation & Ethics

Developing robust regulatory frameworks and ethical guidelines for AI development and deployment.

Comprehensive AI Strategy

Implementing a comprehensive AI strategy to safely harness AI's potential while mitigating its risks.

Collaborative Approach for Equity & Safety

Fostering collaboration between government, private sector, and civil society to ensure technology advances with equity, safety, and well-being for all.

Affordable Healthcare

Ensuring quality care and lower drug costs for all

Democrats are fighting to make healthcare more affordable and accessible for all Americans. Millions have gained coverage through the Affordable Care Act, and efforts continue to lower prescription drug costs, including enabling Medicare to negotiate prices, and expand mental health services.

Accessible & Affordable Care

Ensuring that Americans can access the care they need, building upon the Affordable Care Act to expand coverage.

Lower Prescription Drug Costs

Implementing historic reforms to lower prescription drug prices, including allowing Medicare to negotiate directly with manufacturers.

Expand Mental Health Services

Working to expand access to mental health services as a critical component of overall healthcare.

Protecting Healthcare Gains

Defending against efforts that would strip away healthcare coverage or make it less affordable for American families.

Strengthening Democracy

Protecting voting rights and ensuring fair elections

Democrats are committed to defending and strengthening American democracy by protecting voting rights for all citizens, ensuring elections are free and fair, and making it easier for every voice to be heard. This includes fighting against voter suppression and working to expand access to the ballot box.

Protecting Voting Rights

Focused on protecting and expanding voting rights for all Americans and fighting against voter suppression efforts.

Fair & Free Elections

Working to pass legislation that expands access to the ballot box and ensures that elections are free and fair.

Making Voting Easier

Striving to make it easier, not harder, to vote, upholding the fundamental right of every American to participate in democracy.

Defending Democratic Institutions

Committed to safeguarding the integrity of democratic institutions and processes against threats.
